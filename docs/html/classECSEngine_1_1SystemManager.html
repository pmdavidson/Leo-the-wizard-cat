Detailed Description

template<typename... Components>
class ECSEngine::SystemManager< Components >

Manages ownership and execution order of all registered systems.

and Validity

Systems are owned by SystemManager for its lifetime and are executed in the order they are added. References returned by GetSystem() remain valid until ClearSystems() is called or the manager is destroyed.
