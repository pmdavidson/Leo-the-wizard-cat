Detailed Description

Manages sound resources and audio playback for the ECS engine.

and Validity

Sound buffers and sound instances are stored in unordered_maps and remain valid for the SoundManager's lifetime. Sound names remain valid identifiers for the SoundManager's lifetime. PlaySound() allows multiple sounds to play simultaneously.
